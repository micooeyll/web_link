TaleWorlds Interactive Webinar

Join us for an exclusive webinar with TaleWorlds Interactive, the studio behind the Mount & Blade series. This session will provide valuable insights into the game development industry and how large-scale games are built.

Developers from TaleWorlds will discuss topics such as game engine architecture, gameplay mechanics, and real-world challenges faced during development. Participants will gain a clear understanding of how professional game studios operate.

The webinar will also cover career paths in the gaming industry, including internships, required skills, and opportunities for students in software engineering and game development.

A live Q&A session will be held at the end, giving participants the opportunity to ask questions directly to the developers.
