About ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society. As part of this internationally recognized, US-based organization, our student chapter aims to bring together students passionate about technology, programming, and innovation.

ACM is a student club that brings together individuals passionate about technology and computer science. As part of the internationally recognized US-based Association for Computing Machinery, we aim to learn, build, and grow together.
